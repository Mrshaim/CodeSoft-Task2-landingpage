Help Center

Have questions? We're here to help. Find answers to frequently asked questions below. If you can't find what you're looking for, please don't hesitate to contact us.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the typical timeline for a web development project?

A standard project takes between 6 to 12 weeks from kickoff to launch. This can vary based on the complexity and scope of the project. We provide a detailed timeline after our initial discovery phase.

How do you handle project communication and updates?

We believe in transparent communication. You will have a dedicated project manager and access to a shared workspace (like Slack or Teams) for real-time updates. We also schedule weekly or bi-weekly check-in meetings to review progress and gather feedback.

Do you offer post-launch support and maintenance?

Yes, we offer a range of ongoing support and maintenance retainers to ensure your website remains secure, up-to-date, and performing optimally. We can tailor a package to fit your specific needs.

What technologies do you specialize in?

Our team is proficient in a modern tech stack including React, Node.js, and TypeScript for web development, Swift and Kotlin for native mobile apps, and Figma for UI/UX design. We select the best technology to meet your project's goals.
